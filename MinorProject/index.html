About

RiddlePlay is a fun and educational web app for kids aged 5-12, combining riddles, interactive games, and animations to boost critical thinking and creativity.

Key Features:

Riddles: Daily riddles with themes like "Animals" or "Math Fun," offering hints and reward points for solving.

Games:

Riddle Race: Solve riddles quickly for points.
Puzzle: Solve puzzles to know a logic & answer.
Tic-tae-toe: Use your brain to win the game.
RockPapperScissor:Play and have fun.

Animations:

Unlock short, colorful animations that explain riddle answers, plus animated characters that guide and cheer kids on.

Safe Environment:

No ads, child-friendly design, and progress tracking for parents and teachers. Enhances critical thinking, language skills, problem-solving, memory, and focus, all while ensuring a playful and secure experience.
